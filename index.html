Planet Pit is the sixth studio album by American rapper Pitbull. The album received generally positive reviews from music critics and was a commercial success. The album debuted at number seven on the US Billboard 200, with first-week sales of 55,000 copies in the United States. The album was created with the goal that every song on the album could serve as one single.
