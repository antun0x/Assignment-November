Planet Pit

Released June 2011

Featuring:

  • Vain, Enrique Iglesias
  • T-Pain, Sean Paul, Marc Anthony
  • Chris Brown, Kelly Rowland, Jamie Foxx

Planet Pit is the sixth studio album by Cuban-American rapper Pitbull. The album received generally positive reviews from music critics and was a commercial success. The album debuted at number seven on the US Billboard 200, with first-week sales of 55,000 copies in the United States. The album was created with the goal that every song on the album could serve as one single.

This was Pitbull's final album for the J Records label, since the label would be discontinued during the summer of 2011.

On October 16, 2020, the album was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for combined sales and album-equivalent units of over two million units in the United States.

Global Warming

Released November 2012

Featuring:

  • Christina Aguilera,
  • AFROJACK, Kesha, PLAYB4CK
  • Enrique Iglesias, Kelly Rowland

Global Warming is the seventh studio album recorded by Cuban-American rapper Pitbull. The album received generally positive reviews from music critics and was a commercial success. It debuted at number 14 on the US Billboard 200, selling 64,000 copies in its first week. Global Warming was supported by four singles: "Don't Stop the Party", "Get It Started", "Feel This Moment" and "Back in Time" which was the theme to the 2012 film "Men In Black 3".

In April 2012, it was announced that his seventh studio album would be titled Global Warming. Pitbull announced in May 2012, via Facebook, that the album would be released on November 19, 2012.

The song "Last Night" was originally intended for Paris Hilton.
